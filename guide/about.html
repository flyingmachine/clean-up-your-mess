Clean Up Your Mess: a Guide to Visual Design for Everyone

About

This site was created by Daniel Higginbotham. From this point on, he will be using the first person rather than the third.

The reason I created this site is because I love design, and I wanted to share that love with other people. I want everyone to feel like design is accessible, because I believe it is.

Communication is central to our lives as human beings. In creating this guide, I hope to help others communicate better. I hope that, in my own small way, I'm helping to improve the lives of others.
